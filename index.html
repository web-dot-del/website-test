About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Peak Sunrise Consultants is headquartered in the "Garden City" of Singapore. Leveraging Singapore's strategic position as a global financial and trade hub, we've built an international service network that enables seamless integration of global resources with localized execution.

As a professional consulting firm specializing in high-end advisory services, we focus on three core business areas: Technology Consulting, Economic Consulting, and Policy Consulting. Our approach combines international perspective with local insights to deliver tailored solutions that drive client success.
